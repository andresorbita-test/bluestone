The Material

Born from high altitude and deep pressure.

Lapis lazuli is not just a stone; it is a celestial map. Sourced exclusively from the Flor de los Andes mine at 3,600 meters above sea level, our Chilean lapis is prized for its intense indigo depth and shimmering pyrite inclusions that mirror a starlit mountain sky.
